About Us

Our Journey

ENGG-TECH PTE. LTD. (formerly ENGG-TECH CONSTRUCTION PTE. LTD.) was founded in April 2015 by a team of fire protection professionals with a clear mission — to deliver safety, compliance, and quality without compromise.

What began as a specialist fire protection contractor has grown steadily over the years into a trusted multi-trade contractor serving residential, commercial and industrial clients across Singapore. As our clients' needs evolved, so did our capabilities — expanding into waterproofing, electrical, plumbing and general construction works.

Today, Engg-Tech Pte. Ltd. provides a comprehensive range of building services across Singapore, supporting homeowners, property managers, main contractors and government agencies with reliable, cost-effective solutions. View our completed works on our projects page.

Certifications & Accreditations

Engg-Tech holds industry certifications that reflect our commitment to safety, professionalism and quality workmanship.

› BizSAFE Level 3 — certified under the Workplace Safety and Health Council's BizSAFE programme, demonstrating our commitment to a safe working environment on every project site.

› SCAL Member — registered with the Singapore Contractors Association Ltd (SCAL), Singapore's leading construction industry association.

What We Do

Engg-Tech provides five core service areas across Singapore — all delivered by our own experienced team:

› Fire Protection — sprinkler systems, fire alarm systems, dry riser systems, hose reel systems, fire hydrant systems and suppression systems

› Waterproofing — PU injection grouting, epoxy grouting, basement waterproofing, toilet and wet area waterproofing

› Electrical — wiring, DB board installation, fault finding and renovation electrical works

› Plumbing — pipe repair and replacement, toilet works and renovation plumbing

› General Construction — renovation works, additions and alterations, tiling, painting and general building maintenance

Frequently Asked Questions

When was Engg-Tech founded?
Engg-Tech Pte. Ltd. (formerly Engg-Tech Construction Pte. Ltd.) was founded in April 2015. The company started with a focus on fire protection systems and has since expanded into waterproofing, electrical, plumbing and general construction services across Singapore.

What certifications does Engg-Tech hold?
Engg-Tech is BizSAFE Level 3 certified and a registered member of the Singapore Contractors Association Ltd (SCAL). These certifications reflect our commitment to workplace safety, quality workmanship and industry compliance.

What services does Engg-Tech provide?
Engg-Tech provides fire protection system installation and maintenance, waterproofing and PU injection grouting, electrical works, plumbing works, and general construction services for HDB, condo, commercial and industrial properties across Singapore.

What areas in Singapore does Engg-Tech serve?
Engg-Tech serves all areas across Singapore — HDB estates, condominiums, commercial buildings, industrial facilities and government properties island-wide.

You can request a quotation by emailing info@engg-tech.com or via our contact page. Our team will respond within 1 business day.
