8 Sinaran Drive – Fire Protection and M&E Services

8 Sinaran Drive is a commercial and mixed-use development in Novena, Singapore. 8 Sinaran Drive is located in the Novena area. Commercial and office tenants in this development commonly require fire alarm and sprinkler system maintenance, office reinstatement at lease end and M&E electrical works.

Engg-Tech provides fire protection, waterproofing and M&E contractor services to commercial, industrial and residential properties across Singapore, including the Novena area.

Fire Protection Services for Commercial and Industrial Properties

Singapore's Fire Safety Act requires all commercial and industrial buildings to maintain compliant fire protection systems. Common fire protection works include:

  • Fire sprinkler system (SPK) installation, testing and maintenance
  • Fire alarm system (FA) installation and servicing
  • Dry riser (DR) and wet riser system maintenance
  • Hose reel (HR) inspection and repair
  • Fire hydrant (FH) servicing
  • FM200 and Novec fire suppression systems for server rooms and specialised areas

Office Reinstatement

Commercial tenants vacating office or industrial spaces are required to reinstate the premises to their original condition before returning the keys. Reinstatement works typically include:

  • Hacking and removal of partitions and false ceilings
  • M&E restoration — electrical, ACMV and data cabling
  • Flooring removal and wall patching
  • Repainting to original condition
  • Signage and branding removal
  • Debris disposal and final cleaning

Waterproofing and M&E Maintenance

  • PU injection grouting for water leakage and concrete crack sealing
  • Toilet and wet area waterproofing
  • Electrical troubleshooting and DB box maintenance
  • Plumbing repair and pipe replacement
