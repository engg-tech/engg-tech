Bugis Directory Singapore – Property and Business Services in the Bugis Area

Bugis is a dense commercial and residential area in central Singapore, known for its shopping malls, office buildings, shophouses and mixed-use developments. Businesses and property owners in the Bugis area commonly require fire protection compliance works, office reinstatement services and waterproofing maintenance for older shophouse and commercial buildings.

Fire Protection Services in Bugis

Commercial properties and F&B outlets in the Bugis area are required to maintain compliant fire protection systems. Common requirements include:

  • Fire alarm system (FA) installation and maintenance for retail and F&B units
  • Fire sprinkler system (SPK) installation for commercial spaces
  • Hose reel (HR) and dry riser (DR) servicing for larger buildings
  • FM200 suppression systems for server rooms and electrical rooms
  • Fire safety compliance works for new tenants fitting out a space

Office and Retail Reinstatement

The Bugis area has a high turnover of commercial tenants. When a business vacates a shophouse unit, office or retail space, reinstatement is required before the landlord will return the deposit. Common reinstatement works include partition removal, false ceiling hacking, M&E restoration, repainting and debris disposal.

Shophouse and Building Waterproofing

Older shophouses and buildings in the Bugis area are particularly prone to water seepage through aged concrete, roof membranes and external walls. PU injection grouting and waterproofing membrane works are commonly used to address these issues without full demolition.
