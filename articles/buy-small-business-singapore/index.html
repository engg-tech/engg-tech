Buy Small Business Singapore – Acquiring a Contractor or Property Services SME

Small contractor and property services businesses in Singapore — including fire protection, waterproofing, M&E, renovation and building maintenance companies — are commonly acquired by individuals looking to enter the industry, existing contractors looking to expand their capabilities or investors seeking businesses with stable recurring revenue.

Why Small Contractor Businesses Are Attractive to Buyers

  • Regulatory compliance already in place — a registered contractor business has already obtained its BCA registration, SCDF approvals and BizSAFE certification. These take one to two years to establish from scratch.
  • Existing client base — an established business brings existing relationships with building owners, MCSTs, main contractors and government agencies.
  • Trained workforce — skilled workers are difficult to recruit. Acquiring a business with trained technicians provides immediate operational capacity.
  • Project portfolio — a track record of completed projects supports future tender bids and client credibility.

What to Check Before Buying a Small Contractor Business

  • Are all contractor licences and registrations current and in good standing?
  • Is the revenue dependent on one or two key clients — and will those clients stay after the sale?
  • Are key workers willing to continue with the new owner?
  • Are there outstanding warranty claims, disputes or compliance issues?
  • What is the actual profitability after owner's salary and adjustments?

Typical Small Contractor Business Types Available for Acquisition in Singapore

  • Fire protection installation and maintenance companies
  • Waterproofing and PU grouting specialist contractors
  • M&E electrical and plumbing contracting businesses
  • Renovation and reinstatement contractors
  • Building maintenance and facilities management companies
