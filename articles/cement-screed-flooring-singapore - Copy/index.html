Cement screed flooring is widely used in Singapore for residential, commercial and industrial flooring applications due to its smooth finish, durability and cost-effective performance. Cement screed is commonly applied to create level floor surfaces before tile installation, vinyl flooring, epoxy coating or final floor finishes.
