Class AB Fire Extinguisher Singapore

Class AB fire extinguisher systems are commonly used for protection against ordinary combustible material fires and flammable liquid fire risks. These fire extinguishers are frequently installed in residential, commercial and industrial environments to support fire safety preparedness.

Proper installation, inspection and routine maintenance of fire extinguishers can help improve emergency readiness and long-term operational reliability for fire protection systems.
