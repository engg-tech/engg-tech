Epoxy With Flakes – Decorative Epoxy Flooring Services

Epoxy with flakes flooring systems are commonly used for residential, commercial and industrial spaces due to their decorative appearance, durability and slip-resistant surface finish. Flake epoxy coatings combine coloured flakes with epoxy resin to create a seamless and visually attractive flooring system.

Engg-Tech provides professional epoxy with flakes flooring installation and decorative epoxy coating services for garages, workshops, commercial units, showrooms and industrial facilities.

  • Decorative and modern flooring appearance

  • Slip-resistant flooring finish
  • Easy cleaning and low maintenance
  • Suitable for residential and commercial use
