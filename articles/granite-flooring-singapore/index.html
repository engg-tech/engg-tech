Factors to Consider for Granite Flooring

  • Surface finishing and polishing requirements

Granite flooring systems may experience issues such as scratches, dull surfaces, chipped edges or grout deterioration over time. Proper installation and routine maintenance can help improve flooring durability and long-term appearance.

Routine inspection of flooring conditions, moisture exposure and surface finishing can also help reduce future repair issues and maintain better interior conditions.
