Fire Protection Services

All residential and commercial developments in Singapore are required to maintain compliant fire protection systems. Common fire protection works for condominium and residential developments include:

  • Fire sprinkler system (SPK) inspection and maintenance
  • Fire alarm system (FA) testing and repair
  • Dry riser (DR) and hose reel (HR) servicing
  • Fire hydrant (FH) inspection and maintenance
  • Fire suppression system servicing for carparks and specialised areas

Waterproofing Services

Water leakage is one of the most common maintenance issues in Singapore residential developments. Services commonly required include:

  • Toilet and bathroom waterproofing membrane replacement
  • PU injection grouting for concrete slab cracks and ceiling leaks
  • Epoxy grouting for tile joints in wet areas
  • Roof and external wall waterproofing for landed units
  • Balcony and planter box waterproofing
