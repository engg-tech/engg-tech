Manpower Supply Company Singapore – How to Choose for Construction and M&E Projects

Choosing the right manpower supply company for construction and M&E projects in Singapore affects project quality, safety compliance and timeline. Not all manpower suppliers are the same — some supply general labour while others specifically supply skilled trade workers for fire protection, electrical, plumbing and waterproofing works.

What to Check When Choosing a Manpower Supply Company

  • Trade specialisation — confirm the company can supply workers with the specific skills needed. Fire protection installation, PU grouting and M&E electrical works require different skill sets.
  • Work pass validity — all foreign workers must hold valid work permits or S Passes. Request documentation before deployment.
  • Safety certifications — workers on construction sites must hold valid CSOC (Construction Safety Orientation Course) cards or equivalent. Confirm before site mobilisation.
  • Workmen compensation insurance — the manpower supplier should carry workmen's compensation insurance covering workers during deployment. This protects you from liability.
  • BizSAFE status — companies with BizSAFE certification have established safety management systems. Prefer BizSAFE-certified suppliers for construction and M&E works.

Key Differences Between General Labour and Skilled M&E Workers

General construction labour handles physical site tasks — carrying materials, hacking, cleaning and site preparation. Skilled M&E workers are trained in specific trades:

  • Fire protection technicians install and test sprinklers, alarms and suppression systems
  • Licensed electricians handle distribution boards, wiring and electrical testing
  • Waterproofing specialists apply PU injection, epoxy grouting and membrane systems
  • Pipe fitters handle riser systems, hose reel connections and pressurised pipe installation

Using untrained general workers for skilled M&E tasks creates safety risks, quality failures and compliance issues.

Questions to Ask Before Engaging a Manpower Supplier

  • Do your workers have the required trade certifications for this scope of work?
  • Are all workers covered by workmen's compensation insurance?
  • Can you provide documentation of work pass validity before mobilisation?
  • What is your response time for urgent manpower requirements?
