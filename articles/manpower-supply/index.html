Manpower Supply Singapore – Construction and M&E Project Workforce

Construction, fire protection and M&E projects in Singapore require skilled site workers — pipe fitters, electricians, fire protection technicians, waterproofing applicators and general construction workers — deployed at the right time and in sufficient numbers to meet project schedules. Manpower supply for the construction and M&E sector is about getting the right skilled workers on site, not just general labour.

Types of Workers Commonly Required for M&E and Fire Protection Projects

  • Fire protection technicians — trained in fire sprinkler installation, fire alarm wiring and hose reel system installation
  • Pipe fitters and plumbers — for wet riser, dry riser and fire suppression pipe installation
  • Electricians — for fire alarm panel wiring, emergency lighting and M&E electrical works
  • Waterproofing applicators — skilled in PU injection grouting, epoxy grouting and waterproofing membrane application
  • General construction workers — for hacking, plastering, painting and site preparation works

When Manpower Supply is Needed

  • Large-scale fire protection installation projects requiring multiple crews
  • Tight project timelines needing additional site manpower
  • Shutdown or outage works requiring concentrated workforce deployment
  • Subcontracting arrangements for main contractors needing specialist M&E workers
  • Reinstatement projects with concurrent multi-trade works

Safety and Compliance

All workers deployed on Singapore construction and M&E sites must hold valid work passes, relevant trade certifications and safety inductions. BizSAFE requirements apply to worksites and workers must comply with site safety rules, PPE requirements and method statement procedures.
