Packing Services Singapore – Office Relocation and Reinstatement Support

Packing services in Singapore are commonly needed alongside office relocation and reinstatement works. When a commercial tenant vacates a leased space, they must clear all furniture, equipment and belongings before reinstatement works — hacking, plastering and painting — can begin. Proper packing and clearance planning ensures reinstatement stays on schedule and the security deposit is not held back due to an uncleaned site.

How Packing Fits into Office Reinstatement

Reinstatement works cannot begin until all furniture, equipment and tenant belongings are removed. The typical sequence for an office move-out is:

  1. Engage reinstatement contractor and confirm scope and timeline
  2. Pack and label all furniture, equipment and documents
  3. Arrange movers to transport items to new office or storage
  4. Clear the site completely — reinstatement contractor enters once the space is empty
  5. Reinstatement works proceed — hacking, M&E restoration, painting and cleaning
  6. Handover inspection with landlord

A common cause of reinstatement delays is the site not being cleared in time. Engage packing and moving services at least two to three weeks before the reinstatement start date.

What Packing Services Typically Cover

  • Dismantling and wrapping of office furniture — desks, chairs, cabinets, shelving
  • Packing of office equipment — computers, printers, monitors, servers
  • Document boxing and labeling
  • Wrapping of fragile items — glass panels, screens, decorative pieces
  • Transport to new location or storage facility

Packing for Residential Moving

Residential packing support covers HDB flats, condominiums and landed properties. Items commonly requiring careful packing include:

  • Glassware, crockery and fragile kitchenware
  • Artwork, mirrors and framed photographs
  • Electronic appliances and home entertainment systems
  • Clothing, bedding and soft furnishings
