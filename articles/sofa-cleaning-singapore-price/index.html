Sofa Cleaning Singapore Price – Fabric and Leather Upholstery Cleaning

Sofa cleaning is a common household service in Singapore, particularly for fabric sofas that accumulate dust, stains and odours over time in the humid climate. Prices vary depending on the sofa material, size, condition and cleaning method used. Here is a practical guide to what sofa cleaning typically costs in Singapore and what affects the price.

Typical Sofa Cleaning Prices in Singapore

  • Fabric sofa (2-seater) — approximately $80 to $150
  • Fabric sofa (3-seater) — approximately $120 to $200
  • Fabric sofa (L-shaped) — approximately $180 to $300 depending on size
  • Leather sofa (2-seater) — approximately $100 to $180
  • Leather sofa (3-seater) — approximately $150 to $250
  • Deep stain or odour treatment — additional charge on top of base cleaning price

Note: prices above are indicative. Actual prices depend on the specific cleaner and current market rates.

Factors That Affect Sofa Cleaning Price

  • Material — leather and microfibre require different cleaning solutions and techniques than standard fabric
  • Size and number of seats — larger sofas with more cushions take longer and cost more
  • Stain severity — heavy staining, pet odours or mould require more intensive treatment
  • Cleaning method — steam cleaning typically costs more than dry cleaning but is more effective for deep cleaning
  • Location — some cleaners charge travel fees for areas outside their usual service zones

Sofa Cleaning Methods

  • Steam cleaning — uses hot steam to sanitise, remove dust mites and loosen embedded dirt. Effective for fabric sofas. Requires drying time of a few hours.
  • Dry cleaning — uses dry foam or solvent-based products. Lower moisture content means faster drying time. Suitable for delicate fabrics.
  • Leather conditioning — for leather sofas, cleaning involves a leather-safe cleaner followed by conditioning treatment to restore suppleness and prevent cracking.

When to Clean Your Sofa

A general guideline for Singapore homes is professional sofa cleaning every 12 to 18 months. In households with children, pets or allergies, more frequent cleaning — every 6 to 12 months — is advisable. Regular vacuuming between professional cleans helps extend the time between sessions.
