Temporary Helper Singapore – Site Labour for Renovation and Maintenance Works

Temporary helpers in the construction and property maintenance context are general site workers deployed for short-term tasks that do not require specific trade certifications. They support renovation, reinstatement, moving and general maintenance works — handling physical tasks so that skilled tradespeople can focus on technical work.

What Temporary Helpers Are Used For in Property and Construction

  • Renovation and reinstatement support — carrying materials, clearing debris, assisting with hacking works and site preparation for renovation and reinstatement projects
  • Moving and relocation — packing, loading, carrying and unloading furniture and equipment during office or residential moves
  • Post-renovation cleaning — clearing construction dust, removing waste materials and preparing a space for handover after renovation
  • Warehouse and logistics support — loading and unloading deliveries, organising stored materials and shifting inventory
  • Site support during maintenance works — assisting skilled workers on waterproofing, fire protection or M&E maintenance projects by handling materials and preparation tasks

Difference Between a Temporary Helper and a Skilled Worker

A temporary helper handles general physical tasks — carrying, cleaning, shifting, packing and basic site support. A skilled worker such as a pipe fitter, electrician or waterproofing technician performs technical work requiring trade certification, experience and knowledge of safety and compliance requirements.

For renovation and reinstatement projects, both are typically needed — skilled tradespeople for the technical works and helpers for site logistics, material handling and cleaning.

When to Arrange Temporary Helpers

  • Reinstatement projects with large amounts of debris and demolished material to clear
  • Office relocations requiring furniture dismantling, packing and moving
  • Post-renovation cleaning before handover inspection
  • Renovation projects with heavy material deliveries requiring site logistics support
