Common Causes of Ceiling Leak Problems

  • Roof waterproofing failure
  • Water seepage from upper floors
  • Bathroom or toilet leakage
  • Cracked concrete structures
  • Pipe leakage and plumbing issues
  • External wall water penetration

Common Signs of Ceiling Leakage

  • Water dripping from ceiling
  • Brown water stains
  • Peeling paint and bubbling surfaces
  • Mould and damp patches
  • Cracked or damaged ceiling surfaces
