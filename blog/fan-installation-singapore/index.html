Fan Installation Singapore – Ceiling, Exhaust and Wall Fan Services

Fan installation in Singapore covers ceiling fans, exhaust fans, wall-mounted fans and light-fan combinations for residential and commercial spaces. Proper installation involves correct mounting, load assessment, wiring and testing — not just hanging the fan bracket. A poorly installed fan can wobble, make noise, trip circuit breakers or in the worst case fall from the ceiling.

Types of Fan Installation

  • Ceiling fan installation — most common in Singapore homes. Requires ceiling bracket mounting into a structural point, wiring to the existing switch, blade balancing and speed regulator installation.
  • Exhaust fan installation — used in toilets, kitchens and enclosed rooms. Requires cutting an opening in the wall or ceiling, ductwork connection and electrical wiring to a switch or timer.
  • Wall-mounted fan installation — fixed to walls in commercial kitchens, warehouses or workshops. Requires wall anchor points and electrical connection.
  • Light-fan combination — ceiling fan units with integrated lighting. Requires wiring for both fan speed and light controls, sometimes with separate switches or remote receivers.

Things to Check Before Fan Installation

  • Ceiling material and condition — concrete slab, false ceiling or timber all require different mounting methods. False ceilings need a structural backing point above.
  • Ceiling height — minimum safe clearance from blade tips to floor is generally 2.1 metres. Low ceilings may require a flush-mount or hugger-style fan.
  • Existing wiring — if replacing an old fan, existing wiring should be checked. If installing a new point, new wiring from the distribution board is needed.
  • Fan size and room area — larger rooms require fans with longer blade spans for effective air circulation.

Common Fan Problems We Fix

  • Wobbling or shaking during operation
  • Noisy motor or grinding sounds
  • Faulty speed regulator or remote receiver
  • Fan not starting or tripping the circuit breaker
  • Old fan replacement and new model installation
