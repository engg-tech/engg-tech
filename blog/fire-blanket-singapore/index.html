Fire Blanket Singapore – Safe, Fast & Effective Fire Protection

A fire blanket Singapore solution is one of the most effective tools for stopping small fires quickly. For larger fire protection systems, consider fire alarm systems in Singapore or fire suppression systems. Ideal for homes, restaurants, coffee shops and commercial kitchens, fire blankets allow you to smother flames instantly without chemicals or messy residue.

Engg-Tech supplies and installs SCDF-recommended fire blankets designed for cooking oil fires, small electrical fires, clothing fires and early-stage combustible fires. Each blanket is made from high-temperature woven fibre that cuts off oxygen immediately when placed over the flame.

When to Use a Fire Blanket

  • Kitchen stove fires or cooking oil flare-ups
  • Small electrical appliance fires
  • Clothing fires (wrap safely to extinguish)
  • Small fires in homes, shops or offices

A properly installed fire blanket in Singapore can stop a fire within seconds, minimise property damage and prevent serious injuries. Fire blankets require no training—just pull the tabs, cover the fire and allow it to cool.
