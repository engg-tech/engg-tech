GCB Singapore – Good Class Bungalow Guide Singapore

GCB Singapore refers to Good Class Bungalows, which are considered the most prestigious and exclusive landed residential properties in Singapore.

Good Class Bungalows are located within specially designated Good Class Bungalow Areas and are subject to strict planning guidelines related to land size, building height and development intensity. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

What Is A Good Class Bungalow?

A Good Class Bungalow generally requires a minimum land area of around 1,400 square metres and must be located within designated GCB areas approved by the Urban Redevelopment Authority (URA). :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

  • Large land size requirements
  • Low-density residential environment
  • Strict building height controls
  • Exclusive landed housing zoning
  • Premium residential districts

Popular GCB Areas In Singapore

  • Nassim Road
  • Cluny Road
  • Leedon Park
  • Queen Astrid Park
  • Binjai Park
  • Eng Neo Avenue

Many GCB areas are located within prime districts such as District 10 and District 11, with strict planning controls designed to preserve exclusivity and greenery. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Features Commonly Found In GCB Properties
