HDB Toilet Leaking – Toilet Leakage Repair & Waterproofing

HDB toilet leaking problems are common in older flats and renovated bathrooms where waterproofing systems, pipe joints or tile grout have deteriorated over time. Toilet leakage issues can lead to ceiling stains, paint peeling, mould growth and hidden structural damage if left untreated.

Engg-Tech provides professional HDB toilet leaking repair solutions including waterproofing repair, PU grouting, water leakage injection and toilet seepage rectification for residential properties.

Common signs of toilet leakage include water dripping from ceilings, damp walls, loose tiles, musty smells, cracked grout lines and bubbling paint around toilet areas.

Common Causes of HDB Toilet Leaking

  • Failed toilet waterproof membrane
  • Cracked tile grout and tile joints
  • Pipe leakage or damaged fittings
  • Water seepage through floor slabs
  • Shower area waterproofing failure
  • Improper toilet renovation works

How to Prevent HDB Toilet Leaking Problems
