Injection Grouting Waterproofing – Leak Repair & Crack Sealing

Injection grouting waterproofing is a highly effective method used to stop water leakage and seal cracks in concrete structures. By injecting specialised materials such as polyurethane (PU) or epoxy into cracks, the system penetrates deep into the structure and forms a durable waterproof barrier. For complete building protection, you may also consider fire alarm installation Singapore and fire hose reel systems. This method is widely used in residential, commercial and industrial properties where leakage occurs in walls, ceilings, slabs and basements.

Unlike traditional waterproofing methods that require hacking and surface treatment, injection grouting targets the root cause of leakage internally. PU injection expands to seal active water leaks, while epoxy injection is used for structural crack bonding. Our team selects the appropriate material based on site conditions to ensure effective and long-lasting results.

Why Choose Injection Grouting Waterproofing?

  • Stops active water leakage effectively at the source
  • Minimal hacking with non-invasive repair method
  • Deep penetration into cracks for durable sealing
  • Suitable for walls, ceilings, slabs and underground structures

A properly executed injection grouting waterproofing solution protects your property from water damage, mould growth and structural deterioration.
