Pressure Grouting Concrete Repair – Crack & Structural Repair

Pressure grouting concrete repair is a specialised method used to restore damaged concrete by sealing cracks, filling voids and strengthening structural integrity. By injecting grout materials under controlled pressure, the system penetrates deep into cracks and weak zones, ensuring a solid and durable repair. Depending on the condition of the concrete, different materials such as cementitious grout, polyurethane or epoxy are used. Pressure injection ensures proper distribution within the structure, eliminating voids and restoring strength. This makes it an effective solution for structural cracks, honeycombing, water leakage and foundation repair.
