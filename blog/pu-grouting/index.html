PU Grouting – Polyurethane Injection Waterproofing Solutions

PU grouting is a commonly used waterproofing method for sealing water seepage, concrete cracks and active leakage pathways inside structures and wet areas.

Engg-Tech provides professional PU grouting solutions including polyurethane injection waterproofing, water leakage injection and seepage rectification services for residential, commercial and industrial properties.

PU grouting systems use polyurethane materials that expand and seal hidden leakage pathways inside concrete cracks, joints and structural voids.

Common Applications of PU Grouting

  • Concrete crack waterproofing
  • Basement seepage repair
  • Toilet leakage rectification
  • Roof seepage control
  • Retaining wall waterproofing
  • Lift pit leakage repair

Benefits of PU Grouting

  • Helps seal active water seepage
  • Suitable for concrete structures
  • Can reduce hacking work
  • Deep penetration into cracks and joints
  • Commonly used for non-hacking waterproof repair
  • Supports long-term seepage control

PU grouting is often selected for seepage repair because it can seal hidden leakage pathways without major demolition work in many situations.

Common Signs of Water Seepage Problems

  • Water dripping from walls or ceilings
  • Damp concrete surfaces
  • Cracked walls and slabs
  • Peeling paint and bubbling surfaces
  • Mould and moisture buildup
