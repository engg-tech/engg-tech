PU Injection Grouting Singapore – Fast & Effective Waterproofing Solution

PU injection grouting is one of the most reliable methods to stop water leakage in concrete structures. Instead of applying surface waterproofing, this method seals leaks internally—preventing water from entering at the source.

For professional waterproofing solutions, you may also explore PU grouting services in Singapore.

What is PU Injection Grouting?

PU (polyurethane) injection grouting is a pressure-based waterproofing technique where liquid resin is injected into cracks, joints or voids in concrete. The material reacts with water and expands into foam, creating a strong watertight seal.

Where is PU Injection Grouting Used?

  • Basement wall leakage
  • Ceiling water seepage
  • Toilet and wet area leaks
  • Lift pits and underground structures
  • Concrete cracks and joints

How PU Injection Grouting Works

  1. Identify leakage points
  2. Drill holes along cracks
  3. Install injection packers
  4. Inject PU resin under pressure
  5. Resin expands and seals gaps
  6. Patch surface after completion

Benefits of PU Injection Grouting

  • Stops active water leakage immediately
  • Expands to fill hairline cracks
  • No hacking or major demolition required
  • Works even in wet conditions
  • Cost-effective and long-lasting solution

When Do You Need PU Injection Grouting?

You should consider PU injection grouting if you notice water dripping, damp walls, recurring leakage or cracks in concrete. Early repair prevents further structural damage and reduces long-term costs.
