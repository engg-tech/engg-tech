PU Injection Singapore – Who Needs It and How to Choose a Contractor

PU injection is not a one-size-fits-all solution. Knowing whether your situation actually requires it — and how to find the right contractor — can save time, money and repeated repair attempts.

Who Typically Needs PU Injection in Singapore?

  • HDB owners with recurring ceiling leaks caused by the unit above, particularly at bathroom or kitchen slab areas
  • Condo MCSTs and property managers dealing with basement car park seepage, roof slab leaks or planter box water ingress
  • Landed homeowners with basement walls, retaining walls or flat roofs showing active water seepage
  • Commercial building owners managing lift pit leakage, loading bay slabs or underground utility rooms
  • Contractors and renovation firms needing a waterproofing subcontractor for ongoing projects

When PU Injection Is the Right Choice

PU injection is appropriate when water is actively entering through a concrete crack, joint or structural void — and surface treatments have not resolved the problem. It is also commonly used when hacking is not desired or feasible due to access constraints, tenancy restrictions or the risk of further structural damage.

If the leakage source is unclear, a proper inspection should come before any repair. Injecting into the wrong location wastes material and does not fix the problem.

What to Look for in a PU Injection Contractor

  • Experience with the specific type of structure — HDB, condo, basement or industrial
  • Ability to conduct a proper site assessment before quoting
  • Transparency about the materials used and expected outcome
  • Clear warranty terms for the repair work
  • BizSAFE certification and proper workmen insurance

Questions to Ask Before Starting Work

  • What is the source of the leakage and how was it identified?
  • Which type of PU material will be used and why?
  • How many injection points are needed?
  • What happens if the leakage returns after repair?
  • Is there a warranty and what does it cover?
