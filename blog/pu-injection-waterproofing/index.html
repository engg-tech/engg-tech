PU Injection Waterproofing – Water Leakage & Seepage Repair

PU injection waterproofing is a commonly used method for sealing water seepage, concrete cracks and active leakage pathways inside walls, slabs and structural surfaces. PU injection systems use polyurethane materials to penetrate and seal hidden water intrusion areas.

Engg-Tech provides professional PU injection waterproofing solutions for residential, commercial and industrial properties affected by leakage, seepage and waterproofing failure.

PU injection waterproofing is widely used for toilets, basements, retaining walls, concrete slabs, tunnels, lift pits and wet areas where active water seepage is present.

Common Signs of Water Seepage

  • Water dripping through walls or ceilings
  • Damp concrete and moisture stains
  • Cracked concrete surfaces
  • Peeling paint and bubbling walls
  • Mould and mildew growth
  • Persistent leakage during rain

Applications of PU Injection Waterproofing

  • Concrete crack sealing
  • Toilet leakage repair
  • Basement seepage control
  • Retaining wall waterproofing
  • Lift pit leakage repair
  • Wall and ceiling seepage rectification

PU injection waterproofing is often preferred because it can help reduce hacking work while sealing leakage pathways deep inside structural cracks and voids.
