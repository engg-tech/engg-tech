Roof Leak Detection – Roof Leakage Inspection & Waterproofing

Roof leak detection is important for identifying hidden roof seepage, waterproofing failure and rainwater intrusion before serious structural damage occurs.

Engg-Tech provides professional roof leak detection services including roof leakage inspection, seepage assessment, waterproofing diagnosis and moisture problem identification for residential and commercial properties.

Roof leakage problems can lead to ceiling stains, peeling paint, mould growth and damp walls if water intrusion is not detected and repaired early.

Common Signs of Roof Leakage

  • Ceiling water stains
  • Water dripping during rain
  • Damp ceiling patches
  • Peeling paint and bubbling surfaces
  • Mould and mildew growth
  • Persistent moisture problems indoors

Common Causes of Roof Seepage

  • Damaged waterproof membranes
  • Cracked concrete roof surfaces
  • Blocked drainage systems
  • Failed sealants and joints
  • Weather-related deterioration
  • Improper roof waterproof installation

Proper roof inspection is important because water seepage may travel through hidden pathways before appearing inside ceilings and walls.
