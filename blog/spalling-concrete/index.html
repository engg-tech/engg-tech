Spalling Concrete – Concrete Spalling Repair Services

Spalling concrete refers to the cracking, breaking or flaking of concrete surfaces caused by moisture penetration, reinforcement corrosion and concrete deterioration over time.

Engg-Tech provides professional spalling concrete repair solutions including concrete patch repair, exposed reinforcement treatment, ceiling concrete repair and structural maintenance services.

Concrete spalling problems are commonly found in ceilings, balconies, beams, columns and external wall structures exposed to moisture and weather conditions.

Common Causes of Spalling Concrete

  • Water seepage into concrete structures
  • Corrosion of reinforcement bars
  • Concrete aging and deterioration
  • Poor waterproof protection
  • Moisture and humidity exposure
  • Structural movement and cracking

Common Signs of Spalling Concrete

  • Cracked concrete surfaces
  • Loose or falling concrete pieces
  • Exposed reinforcement bars
  • Rust stains on ceilings or walls
  • Concrete surface bulging

Proper site inspection is important before starting repair works because different concrete conditions may require different repair methods and waterproofing solutions.
