Swimming Pool Waterproofing – Pool Leakage & Seepage Repair

Swimming pool waterproofing is important for preventing pool leakage, concrete seepage and water loss caused by damaged waterproof membranes, cracks and deteriorated pool surfaces.

Engg-Tech provides professional swimming pool waterproofing solutions including pool leakage repair, waterproof membrane systems, crack sealing and seepage rectification services for residential and commercial properties.

Swimming pools are constantly exposed to water pressure and moisture conditions, making proper waterproof protection essential for long-term durability and water retention.

Common Signs of Swimming Pool Leakage

  • Unexplained water level reduction
  • Cracks on pool surfaces
  • Water seepage around pool areas
  • Damp surrounding walls or flooring
  • Loose pool tiles and grout
  • Persistent moisture around concrete structures
