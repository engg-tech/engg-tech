Common Signs of Waterproofing Failure

  • Roof water leakage during rain
  • Ceiling dampness and water stains
  • Concrete cracks and seepage marks
  • Peeling paint and mould growth
  • Persistent moisture problems

Proper surface preparation and waterproof inspection are important before installing waterproof membrane systems. The condition of the existing surface can affect waterproof performance and long-term durability.
