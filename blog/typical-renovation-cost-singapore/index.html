Typical Renovation Cost Singapore – Renovation Cost Guide

Typical renovation cost Singapore depends on property type, renovation scope, material selection, carpentry requirements and whether the property is a new unit or resale property.

In Singapore, renovation costs for HDB flats, condos and landed homes can vary significantly depending on hacking works, plumbing replacement, electrical upgrading and custom interior requirements.

According to multiple Singapore renovation cost guides, a basic renovation may start from around S$20,000 to S$40,000 while larger or premium projects can exceed S$100,000. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Typical HDB Renovation Cost Singapore

  • 3-room HDB: around S$20,000 to S$45,000+
  • 4-room HDB: around S$35,000 to S$65,000+
  • 5-room HDB: around S$45,000 to S$90,000+

Resale HDB renovations usually cost more because they may require hacking, rewiring, replumbing and waterproofing rectification works. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Typical Condo Renovation Cost Singapore

  • New condo renovation: around S$15,000 to S$50,000+
  • Resale condo renovation: around S$50,000 to S$130,000+

Condo renovation costs are often higher due to MCST requirements, restricted working hours and additional approval procedures. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Typical Landed Renovation Cost Singapore

  • Basic landed renovation: around S$80,000+
  • Major landed renovation: around S$200,000+
  • Full rebuilding projects: S$600,000+

Landed property renovation costs can increase significantly depending on structural works, extensions, reconstruction and design complexity. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Common Renovation Cost Components

  • Carpentry and custom cabinets
  • Flooring and tiling works
  • Electrical rewiring
  • Plumbing replacement
  • Painting and ceiling works
  • Waterproofing and hacking works

Carpentry is commonly one of the largest renovation cost components and may account for a substantial portion of the total renovation budget. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Factors Affecting Renovation Cost

  • Property size and layout
  • New or resale condition
  • Material and finish quality
  • Amount of hacking work
  • Custom carpentry requirements
  • Project timeline and complexity
