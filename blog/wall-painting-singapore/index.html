Wall Painting Singapore – Interior and Exterior Painting Services

Wall painting is one of the most cost-effective ways to refresh a property. A proper paint job — done with correct surface preparation, the right paint type and clean workmanship — can transform a space and protect walls from moisture, staining and wear. Done poorly, paint peels within months.

Interior Wall Painting

Interior painting in Singapore covers living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, toilets and office spaces. The process involves:

  • Patching cracks, holes and uneven plaster surfaces
  • Sanding and priming bare or previously painted surfaces
  • Masking edges, fixtures and flooring before painting
  • Applying two to three coats of interior emulsion paint
  • Clean finishing at corners, skirting and ceiling lines

Paint selection matters. Bathrooms and kitchens need moisture-resistant paint. High-traffic areas benefit from washable or semi-gloss finishes. Feature walls may use different sheens or textures to create depth.

Exterior Wall Painting

Exterior painting for landed properties and building facades requires weatherproof exterior paint that can withstand Singapore's rain, heat and UV exposure. Preparation is more intensive — surfaces must be cleaned, fungal growth treated and cracks filled before painting. Exterior paint typically lasts five to eight years before repainting is needed.

When Painting is Part of Larger Works

Wall painting is often the final step in renovation, reinstatement or waterproofing works. After hacking, plastering and patching, walls need repainting to restore a clean finish. After ceiling leak repairs, the affected ceiling and surrounding wall area needs repainting once the leak is confirmed stopped.
