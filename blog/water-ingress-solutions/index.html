Water Ingress Solutions – Seepage & Waterproofing Repair

Water ingress solutions are important for controlling water seepage, moisture penetration and leakage problems affecting walls, ceilings, floors and concrete structures. Water ingress can lead to mould growth, peeling paint, ceiling stains and long-term structural deterioration if left untreated.

Engg-Tech provides professional water ingress solutions including waterproofing repair, PU injection grouting, water leakage injection and seepage rectification services for residential, commercial and industrial properties.

Water ingress problems commonly occur in bathrooms, rooftops, balconies, basements, concrete slabs and external walls exposed to rain and moisture.

Common Signs of Water Ingress

  • Water stains on ceilings and walls
  • Damp surfaces and peeling paint
  • Mould and mildew growth
  • Cracked concrete and wall seepage
  • Loose tiles and damaged grout
  • Persistent moisture problems during rain

Common Causes of Water Ingress

  • Failed waterproof membranes
  • Cracked concrete and wall joints
  • Roof leakage and drainage problems
  • Deteriorated sealants and grout
  • External wall rain penetration
  • Aging building structures

Proper inspection is important before selecting the most suitable waterproofing and seepage repair method. Different leakage conditions may require different repair systems depending on the source and severity of the problem.
