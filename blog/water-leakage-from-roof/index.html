Water Leakage From Roof – Roof Seepage & Waterproof Repair

Water leakage from roof problems can lead to ceiling stains, damp walls, peeling paint and long-term structural damage if not repaired early. Roof seepage commonly occurs due to failed waterproof membranes, cracked concrete, drainage issues and weather exposure.

Engg-Tech provides professional water leakage from roof repair solutions including roof waterproofing, PU injection grouting, water leakage injection and seepage rectification services for residential and commercial properties.

Roof leakage issues are commonly noticed during heavy rain when water penetrates through cracks, joints and damaged waterproof layers.

Common Causes of Water Leakage From Roof

  • Damaged waterproof membrane
  • Cracked roof concrete surfaces
  • Blocked roof drainage systems
  • Failed sealants and expansion joints
  • Weather-related deterioration
  • Poor waterproof installation work
