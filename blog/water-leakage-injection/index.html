Water Leakage Injection – Waterproofing & Leak Repair

Water leakage injection is a proven waterproofing method used to stop leaks and seal cracks within concrete structures. By injecting specialised materials such as polyurethane (PU) or epoxy into affected areas, the system penetrates deeply and forms a durable barrier against water ingress. Unlike traditional waterproofing approaches that rely on surface treatment, water leakage injection targets the source of the problem internally. PU injection expands to seal active leaks, while epoxy injection bonds structural cracks. This ensures a long-lasting solution without major hacking or disruption.

  • Minimal hacking with non-invasive repair method
  • Deep penetration into cracks for durable waterproofing
