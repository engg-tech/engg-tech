Water Seepage Repair Method – Waterproofing & Leakage Solutions

Water seepage repair method selection depends on the source of leakage, severity of moisture intrusion and the condition of the affected structure. Water seepage problems can occur in walls, ceilings, floors, toilets, rooftops and concrete structures exposed to moisture and rainwater.

Engg-Tech provides professional water seepage repair method solutions including PU injection grouting, waterproof injection, crack sealing and seepage rectification services for residential and commercial properties.

Early repair is important because prolonged water seepage may lead to mould growth, peeling paint, structural deterioration and hidden moisture damage.

Common Water Seepage Problems

  • Ceiling water leakage
  • Wall seepage and damp patches
  • Bathroom waterproofing failure
  • Concrete crack leakage
  • Roof seepage during rain
  • Floor and tile leakage problems

Common Water Seepage Repair Methods

  • PU injection grouting
  • Water leakage injection
  • Waterproof membrane repair
  • Concrete crack sealing
  • Sealant replacement
  • Surface waterproof coating

PU grouting is one of the most commonly used repair methods for active seepage because polyurethane materials can penetrate cracks and seal hidden leakage pathways inside concrete structures.

Factors Affecting Repair Method Selection

  • Severity of water seepage
  • Location of leakage source
  • Condition of existing waterproofing
  • Building structure and accessibility
  • Need for hacking or non-hacking repair

Some seepage problems can be repaired using localized waterproof treatment methods without major demolition work. Proper inspection is important before selecting the most suitable waterproof repair solution.
