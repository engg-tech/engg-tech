Window Leak Repair – Window Water Seepage & Waterproofing

Window leak repair is important for preventing rainwater seepage, wall dampness, peeling paint and moisture damage caused by leaking window frames and deteriorated sealants.

Engg-Tech provides professional window leak repair solutions including window waterproofing, sealant replacement, crack sealing and water seepage rectification for residential and commercial properties.

Window leakage problems commonly occur during heavy rain when water penetrates through damaged sealants, frame gaps, wall cracks and deteriorated waterproofing areas around windows.

Common Causes of Window Leakage

  • Damaged window sealants
  • Cracks around window frames
  • Rainwater seepage through walls
  • Deteriorated waterproofing systems
  • Improper window installation
  • Building movement and aging structures
