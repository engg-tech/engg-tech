About Us

A Decade of
Doing It Right.

ENGG-TECH PTE. LTD. (formerly Engg-Tech Construction Pte. Ltd.) was founded in April 2015 by fire protection professionals with a clear mission — competitive pricing, trusted quality and timely completion.

From a specialist fire protection contractor, we have grown into a full-service multi-trade company trusted by residential, commercial and industrial clients across Singapore. Our capabilities today span waterproofing, electrical, plumbing and general construction — all managed under one accountable roof.

Whether you own an HDB flat, manage a condominium estate or operate a commercial facility, Engg-Tech has the credentials, experience and workforce to get the job done properly — the first time.
