About Us

Our Journey

ENGG-TECH PTE. LTD. (formerly Engg-Tech Construction Pte. Ltd.) was founded in April 2015 by fire protection professionals with a clear mission — competitive pricing, trusted quality and timely completion.

From a specialist fire protection contractor, we have grown into a trusted multi-trade contractor serving residential, commercial and industrial clients across Singapore — expanding into waterproofing, electrical, plumbing and general construction.
