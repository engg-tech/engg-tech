The Process

How PU Grouting Works

Polyurethane resin is injected directly into cracks under pressure — it expands on contact with moisture, forming a watertight seal from within.

1

Free Site Inspection

Our specialist identifies the source of leakage, assesses severity and recommends the right injection points — at no cost to you.

2

Drill Injection Ports

Small holes (10–12mm) are drilled at strategic intervals along the crack or leak point. No major hacking or structural damage involved.

3

High-Pressure PU Injection

Polyurethane resin is pumped in under pressure using specialist equipment. It expands up to 20x its volume, filling all voids and cracks from inside out.

4

Cure, Seal & Finish

The expanded resin cures into a flexible, rubber-like plug. Ports are sealed and the surface is made good — job done in hours, not days.

5

Warranty Issued

Every PU grouting job comes with a 1-year workmanship warranty. If it leaks again, we come back and fix it — no questions asked.

Common Questions

PU Grouting FAQ

How long does PU grouting take?
Most residential PU grouting jobs are completed within 2–6 hours depending on the extent of the leakage. Commercial or larger-scale projects may take 1–2 days. There is minimal drying time — the area is typically usable immediately after completion.
How much does PU grouting cost in Singapore?
Cost varies depending on the area affected, severity of leakage and accessibility. We provide a free site assessment and transparent quotation before any work begins. WhatsApp us with photos of the affected area for a ballpark estimate.
Is PU grouting a permanent solution?
Yes — when correctly applied, PU grouting creates a permanent, flexible seal within the crack or void. The polyurethane material remains elastic after curing, accommodating minor structural movement without cracking. We back every job with a 1-year workmanship warranty.
Can PU grouting stop active, flowing water leaks?
Yes — this is one of PU grouting's greatest advantages. Polyurethane foam reacts immediately with water and expands to fill cracks and stop active flowing leaks. Unlike other methods, the area does not need to be dry beforehand.
Do I need to vacate my home during the works?
In most cases, no. PU grouting involves minimal noise and disruption. There is no major hacking or demolition. Residents can typically remain in the unit during works, though we recommend staying clear of the immediate work area.
Do you service HDB, condo and commercial properties?
Yes — Engg-Tech handles PU grouting across all property types in Singapore including HDB flats, private condominiums, landed property, commercial buildings, industrial facilities and government projects.
