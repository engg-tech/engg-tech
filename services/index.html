PU Injection Grouting (Waterproofing)

We provide professional PU injection grouting services in Singapore to stop water leakage in concrete structures. This method seals cracks internally, preventing water from entering at the source instead of just covering the surface.

› Ceiling water leakage repair

› Basement and retaining wall leakage

› Toilet and wet area seepage

› Concrete crack injection sealing

› Lift pit and underground waterproofing

Our PU grouting Singapore solutions are fast, non-invasive, and effective for both residential and commercial properties.

Epoxy Grouting (Tile & Waterproofing)

We provide professional epoxy grout Singapore services for tile grout repair, replacement, and waterproofing in residential and commercial properties. This method creates a durable, non-porous seal that prevents water penetration, stains, and bacteria buildup.

› Toilet grout repair and epoxy waterproofing

› Bathroom and shower tile grout replacement

› Floor tile grout and wall tile grout restoration

› Grout filler replacement for damaged or cracked joints

Our epoxy tile grout solutions are long-lasting, easy to maintain, and ideal for improving hygiene and durability in tiled surfaces across Singapore.
