PU Grouting Machine & Process

We use specialized PU grouting machine systems to inject polyurethane resin directly into cracks. The material expands upon contact with water, sealing voids and stopping leaks effectively.

This water leakage injection process is precise and minimizes disruption to existing structures.

Our services also include chemical infusion waterproofing, which works alongside PU injection to strengthen and seal affected areas. This method is ideal for persistent leakage conditions where traditional waterproofing methods are insufficient.
