PU Grouting Singapore Water Leakage Repair Before After

This video shows Engg-Tech's PU injection grouting process in action — stopping active water leakage through concrete walls without hacking. The before and after results demonstrate how effective PU grouting Singapore solutions can seal cracks and seepage fast.

The technique involves injecting polyurethane resin directly into cracks under pressure. The material expands on contact with water, filling voids and forming a watertight seal — suitable for HDB, condo, and commercial properties.
